About the Farm

Located on 10 scenic acres in North Durham, we aim to provide you with fresh, delicious home-grown produce and handmade treats. Owned and operated by Maggie and Kevin Healy since 2004, our farm features pesticide-free gardens, a beautiful pond, and space to roam.
